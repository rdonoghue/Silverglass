  • Drive - Revenge
    Following the "Bad thing" that ended up scattering or murdering our kin (I don't recall if that was defined. Maybe Winter Queen came up or something. We can Lean into Jax here). Fabien basically Inigo Montoya'ed himself with a self-promise to one day take care of that. Off to study. Learned some sneaky damned assissing stuff in the Feywild for a time, but some material plane job (to be named as story), got him noticed by one of the governments (Leaving which one to you, if helpful). Trained further as solo sort of operative. Basically a history of getting some of the best possible training for years to turn him into a very specific sort of killing monster that ideally nobody ever sees coming. He has since left the job, although, nobody every truly gets "out" of the business.

    • What do you do when you’re not adventuring?
      Fabien bounces around to various normal odd jobs to get cash. Things that don't show off his skills, like working as a dock hand, or a dish washer. Average punch-clock joe stuff.
    • Why is that not what you do all the time?
      He maintains a low profile to avoid former handlers looking to drag him back for one more job, or in case he's ever burned and some former victims come looking. He's trained in how to stay off the radar, so he's not taking home prize money from archery contests. He also keeps an ear to the ground in case any word pops up about his lifelong target.
      Previous career set him up with enough gear that he could sell and retire in comfort. But he needs to stay sharp just in case. He might get a revenge lead that would cause him to just walk off a job and leave the city for a bit, or go on a stakeout, or whatever. So far, they've all turned up to be useless, and he shows back up at some other job. He also just can't say no to family, and the few close friends might as well be family.. See #4, #5 for more detail).
    • What is the most likely reason you’ll make a bad decision?
      Aside from his own revenge. Going freelance, and keeping an ear out in low places means he hears stories. Stories about people who have been wronged. The sort of people who just won't ever be in a position to do something about it. Fabien is a sucker for nearly anyone with the right sad story. Some son or daughter who's poor father was imprisoned for some imagined insult to a noble? He'll take that job pro-bono. They don't always end in murder. Sometimes a creative punishment is more fitting, but it's coming. That's what he's willing to risk exposure for. That and…
    • Name one person you’d go out on a limb for
      Jax for sure. We are actual brothers rather than just Harengon-related. Fabien is a bit older, but may appear the same age or younger due to some number of material-plane years in the Feywild. Presuming time is similarly skewed. But he has a brother, who sometimes needs a hand, or a little help. Additionally, Fabien is slow to trust, and add friends. Once he does though, he considers them chosen family and will also easily drop anything short of his personal mission to help them out.
